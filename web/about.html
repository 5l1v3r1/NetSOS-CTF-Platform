NetSOS CTF Platform is a web platform for Jeopardy-style Capture the Flag managed by Network, Security, & Operating System Special Interest Group, Ristek Fakultas Ilmu Komputer Universitas Indonesia.. This platform is based on picoCTF Platform 2 with additonal features like multiple-contest management and option for choosing between individual or team registration.

This platform is under development and have a standalone repository.
